# Welcome to the DadyTube Wiki

**DadyTube** is the "Digital Sandbox" – a nourishing, safe, and tactile entertainment platform designed from the ground up to protect children's vision, encourage self-regulation, and provide a premium, tactile experience.

This wiki provides detailed documentation for both **Parents/Users** and **Developers**. Choose a topic below to learn more about how DadyTube works under the hood and how to make the most of its features.
